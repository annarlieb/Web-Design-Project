ExperieN(Y)Ce New York City!

New York City is a city like no other.

New York City consists of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Staten Island, and the Bronx. As of 2020, its population is a massive 8.8 million people. New York City is the world’s premier financial centre - yet it’s so much more! New York City is the home to culture and fun!

With loads of tourist attractions, from parks to museums, New York City has it all.

Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned New Yorker, there’s always something new to discover (or experien(y)ce) in the Big Apple.

When should I visit?
New York City never stops, so come whenever fits your schedule! However, you may want to keep the following considerations in mind when choosing which season to visit.

Winter:

There’s nothing like New York City after snowfall.
December | Average High: 5°C (41°F) | Average Low: -1°C (30°F)
January | Average High: 4°C (39°F) | Average Low: -3°C (26°F)
February | Average High: 5°C (40°F) | Average Low: -3°C (27°F)

Spring:

The trees get their leaves back, flowers are blooming, and New York City looks spectacular!
March | Average High: 8°C (48°F) | Average Low: 1°C (34°F)
April | Average High: 16°C (61°F) | Average Low: 6°C (44°F)
May | Average High: 21°C (71°F) | Average Low: 11°C (53°F)

Summer:

New York City in the summer can get quite hot, but is still incredible!
June | Average High: 27°C (81°F) | Average Low: 17°C (63°F)
July | Average High: 29°C (85°F) | Average Low: 18°C (68°F)
August | Average High: 28°C (83°F) | Average Low: 19°C (66°F)

Fall:

Seeing the leaves change to vibrant colors is unforgettable!
September | Average High: 25°C (77°F) | Average Low: 16°C (60°F)
October | Average High: 19°C (67°F) | Average Low: 10°C (51°F)
November | Average High: 12°C (54°F) | Average Low: 5°C (41°F)
