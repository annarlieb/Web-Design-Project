Special Events in New York City


There are many special events that happen throughout the year. Plan your trip around one of these events to have a truly unforgettable experience!

Ball Drop

When: 1 January 12:00 am

Where: Times Square

What: New Year's Celebration

First Year: 1907

Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade

When: Thanksgiving morning (the last Thursday in November)

Where: Central Park West to Macy's Herald Square

What: Parade with floats and HUGE helium balloons

First Year: 1924

Radio City Rockettes Christmas Spectacular

When: Mid November - Early January

Where: Radio City Music Hall

What: Musical

First Year: 1933

Governors Ball Music Festival

When: Early June

Where: Randall's Island

What: Music Festival

First Year: 2011
